# Shield Insurance Analysis | Power BI Dashboard

This repository contains a **Power BI analytics project** focused on analyzing customer growth, revenue performance, and policy trends for **Shield Insurance**.  
The goal of this project is to provide stakeholders with a **centralized, interactive dashboard** that enables better monitoring of business performance and customer behavior across different demographics, cities, and sales channels.

---

# 🏢 About the Company

**Shield Insurance** provides affordable and customizable insurance products designed to serve individuals across various age groups.

The company operates in multiple major Indian cities including:

- Mumbai  
- Chennai  
- Delhi  
- Hyderabad  
- Indore  

Customers can purchase policies through several channels:

- Offline insurance agents  
- Direct sales teams  
- Mobile application  
- Company website  

This multi-channel strategy ensures wide accessibility and convenience for customers.

---

# 📌 Problem Statement

Shield Insurance lacked a **consolidated and interactive analytical view** to monitor key business metrics such as:

- Customer growth
- Revenue performance
- Policy adoption trends

Additionally, there was **limited visibility into how customer behavior differed across demographics**, including:

- Age groups
- Cities
- Sales channels
- Policy types

This lack of insights made it difficult for decision makers to:

- Identify growth opportunities
- Evaluate sales channel effectiveness
- Understand demographic impacts on revenue and settlement expectations

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

The primary goals of this analysis include:

- Track **total customers and total revenue**
- Monitor **daily revenue growth rate** and **daily customer growth rate**
- Analyze **month-over-month policy performance**
- Segment customers by **age group and city**
- Compare **revenue trends with customer growth trends**
- Evaluate **sales channel performance**
- Analyze **age-group behavior**, including:
  - Policy preference
  - Sales mode preference
  - Expected settlement percentage

# 📊 Key Insights

## Overall Business Snapshot

- **Total Revenue:** 989.25M  
- **Total Customers:** 26,841  
- **Average Daily Revenue:** 5.47M  
- **Average Daily Customers:** 148  

---

## 🌆 City-Level Insights

- **Delhi NCR**
  - Highest revenue: **401.57M**
  - Highest customers: **11,007**

- **Indore**
  - Lowest revenue: **81.35M**
  - Lowest customers: **2,096**

- **Delhi & Mumbai**
  - Only cities with **1,000+ agents across all sales channels**

- **Indore**
  - Weak agent distribution across most channels  
  - Offline agents remain dominant (1,176 agents)

- **Monthly Performance**
  - **March:** Peak month  
    - Revenue: 264M  
    - Customers: 7,081

  - **November:** Lowest performing month  
    - Revenue: 132M  
    - Customers: 3,787

---

## 👥 Age Group Insights

- **31–40 Age Group**
  - Highest revenue: **309.32M**
  - Highest customers: **10,431**

- **50+ Segment**
  - Strong customer base: **11,400 customers**
  - Highest settlement percentage  
  - Age 65+ settlement rate: **74.33%**

- **Under-30 Segment (18–24 & 25–30)**
  - Lowest revenue:
    - 24.94M
    - 56.73M
  - Lowest customers:
    - 1,822

- **Settlement Rate Trend**
  - Settlement percentage increases with age
  - From **37.51% (18–24)** to **74.33% (65+)**

# 🚀 Strategic Recommendations

| Focus Area | Recommended Action |
|-----------|-------------------|
| High Value Segment | Strengthen focus on **31–40 age group**, the strongest revenue driver |
| Customer Retention | Expand engagement with **50+ customers**, who show high settlement trust |
| Youth Market | Reposition policies for **under-30 customers** to improve adoption |
| Sales Channel Strategy | Continue investing in **Offline Agent network** |
| Digital Growth | Improve **website experience and online purchase flow** |
| Policy Optimization | Optimize pricing and marketing for **POL2005HEL** |
| Product Strategy | Revamp **POL4321HEL** due to high customers but low revenue |
| Market Expansion | Focus on **Tier-2 markets like Indore** |
| Sales Strategy | Replicate **Delhi NCR sales model** across other cities |
| Seasonal Marketing | Launch targeted campaigns during **November slowdown** |
| Peak Month Strategy | Replicate **March promotional strategies** across the year |
